Wellcome to
Carolina Point Resort

Carolina Point Resort is a modern retreat Guesthouse offering spacious living in a tropical garden setting with a large patio overlooking the garden. Carolina Point Resort is just 7 minutes walk from the Airport with its back yard touching the caribbean Sea at the entrance to the iconic Pigeon Point Beach in Tobago. The woody area is well known for its beauty, beaches, and concentration of fruit trees, flowers and fauna.
